About

Proteins interact with each other and organize themselves into macromolecular machines (ie. complexes) to carry out essential functions of the cell. We have a good understanding of a few complexes such as the proteasome and the ribosome but currently we have an incomplete view of all protein complexes as well as their functions. The plant.MAP attempts to address this lack of understanding by integrating several large scale protein interaction datasets to obtain the most comprehensive view of protein complexes.
